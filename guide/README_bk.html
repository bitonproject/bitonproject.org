# biton, the Internet potluck

We celebrate networks as a safe space, where we can communicate and express ourselves free of control, censorship or surveillance. And you are invited!

biton joins the long-running efforts to create decentralized networking infrastructures, where users communicate directly with one another without the need . Instead, It is all about empowering digital sovereignty for individuals and communities.

biton proposes an information-centric, service-oriented networking model built around the concept of swarms: local clusters of nodes that gather around a common resource or service. For instance, a swarm could be the viewers of an online conference stream, or a shared repository with open data in the context of a neighborhood. Effectively, biton leverages the emergent trust in these utility networks to create a social net and thwart adversaries from isolating individuals (eclipse attacks).

At the same time, swarms reinforce digital sovereignty: content remains local to the participants of the swarm and is not shared with third parties, thus giving users better control over their data. In fact, trust is built from the bottom up, and the trust relationships are leveraged both by the network operations, and by applications that include the biton module (opens new window).
